About Us

This alumni website aims to provide all graduates of the Department of Computer Science (UCE,OU) with efficacious connections to fellow alumni, the department, and to current students. In virtue of Osmania University's initial post graduate programme in Computer Science which started in 1981, this department has alumni page today has the potential to reach and connect to nearly 5000 alumni around the world. This website will serve as a bridge to establish mutually beneficial relationships between the department, it's graduates, and it's students. With a mission of advancing and promoting the interests of the department, We seek to reach, serve and engage all Dept. Of CSE alumni and students, to foster a lifelong intellectual and emotional connection between the department and it's graduates, and to provide the department with goodwill and support. All in all, this website aims to advance and promote the interests of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, UCE, OU by connecting alumni and inspiring them to support the university by:



        1.Volunteering & Participating in department and alumni events.
        2.Acting as ambassadors and advocates on behalf of the department.
        3.Giving back to the department.
